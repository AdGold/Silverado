SILVERADO CINEMA

Welcome to Silverado!

Here at Silverado, we're committed to giving you a viewing experience that you will enjoy, from standard viewing in our Cinema Rivola to 3D films and beanbag and first class seating in our newly renovated cinema Maxima, we offer a high quality viewing experience for all.

Come to our Cinema Rivola to see some wider culture with regular foreign language films and art house films showing.

Are you looking for a theatre for a private function? Cinema Rivola is also available for hire to the local community. Please use the contact page if you are interested in hiring Cinema Rivola for a private function
